About Us

We come from a family of immigrants. We understand the struggles that come with starting a new life in America. Our parents came here as teenagers and had to not only deal with the struggles of discrimination, but also language barriers and a lack of oppurtunity. That is why we are committed to ensuring that all immigrants have the opportunity to express themselves and pursue the American Dream. We hope to spread the message and show why diversity is so important. Help us make the American Dream a reality for everyone!
